AIRY AUTOMOTIVE MANUFACTURING INDUSTRY

Airy Automotive will be part of the Automotive Manufacturing industry. We specialize in developing new and more efficient powertrains. Currently, there is a shift in the automotive sector thus switching from Internal Combustion engines to electric motors. Airy Automotive is in Lima, Ohio, United States. Currently, the only person in charge of operations for Airy Automotive is the business owner, Wilhelm Von Effah Henkel, who spends about 40 hours a week in research and business development. Airy Automotive is currently in the developmental stages. Airy Automotive is planning to be one of the world leaders in automotive powertrain development. As we continue to grow, we shall make our services available to other companies like powertrain. Founded by a guy who started by scribbling their ideas on a piece of paper.
